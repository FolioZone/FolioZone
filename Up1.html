Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FolioZone.

Our turnaround time depends on the package you choose:

  • Starter: 48 hours
  • Basic: 24 hours
  • Premium: 12 hours (priority service)

These times start from when we receive all your information and materials.

Yes! Each package includes a certain number of updates:

  • Starter: No updates included (can purchase separately)
  • Basic: 3 updates within 6 months
  • Premium: 10 updates within 1 year

After your included updates are used, additional updates can be purchased at a small fee.

Currently, all portfolios are hosted on our subdomain (folio-zone.com/your-name). We're working on custom domain support which will be available soon as an add-on feature.

If you need a custom domain urgently, please contact us and we'll discuss possible solutions.

We accept various payment methods for your convenience:

  • Credit/Debit cards (Visa, MasterCard)
  • Bank transfers (Jordanian banks)
  • Mobile payment apps (Zain Cash, Orange Money)
  • Cash on delivery (Amman area only)

Payment is required before we start working on your portfolio.

We want you to be completely satisfied with your portfolio. Here's our process:

  1. We'll send you a preview link when your portfolio is ready
  2. You can request up to 2 rounds of revisions (for Basic and Premium packages)
  3. If you're still not satisfied, we offer a full refund within 7 days

Our goal is to create something you'll be proud to share with potential employers and clients.
