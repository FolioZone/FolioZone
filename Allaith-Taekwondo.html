About Us

من نحن

For over 40 years, Allaith Taekwondo Center has been shaping champions both on and off the mat. Founded in 1985, our center has become a beacon of excellence in martial arts training in Jordan.

Our mission is to develop disciplined, confident individuals through the art of Taekwondo while maintaining a strong focus on academic excellence and personal development.

We believe that true champions are built through perseverance, respect, and continuous self-improvement. Our students learn valuable life skills that extend far beyond the dojang.

لأكثر من 40 عامًا، كان مركز الليث للتايكواندو يصنع الأبطال على الحصيرة وخارجها. تأسس المركز عام 1985، وأصبح منارة للتميز في تدريب الفنون القتالية في الأردن.

مهمتنا هي تطوير أفراد منضبطين وواثقين من خلال فن التايكواندو مع الحفاظ على تركيز قوي على التميز الأكاديمي والتطور الشخصي.

نؤمن بأن الأبطال الحقيقيين يُبنون من خلال المثابرة والاحترام والتطوير الذاتي المستمر. يتعلم طلابنا مهارات حياتية قيمة تتجاوز بكثير حدود الدوجانج.

Founder & Head Coach

المؤسس والمدرب الرئيسي

Grand Master Fouad Jarwan

9th Dan Black Belt - Kukkiwon Certified (2024)

With over 45 years of experience in Taekwondo, Grand Master Jarwan is one of the most respected martial arts instructors in the Middle East.

He began his Taekwondo journey in 1980 and established Allaith Taekwondo Center in 1985 with the vision of creating a world-class training facility that nurtures both athletic and personal excellence.

In 2024, he was awarded the prestigious 9th Dan Black Belt from Kukkiwon, the World Taekwondo Headquarters in South Korea, recognizing his lifetime dedication to the art.
