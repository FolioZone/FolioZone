Professional Summary

Senior engineering professional with over 20 years of leadership in municipal infrastructure development and public sector governance in Palestine. Pioneered systemic improvements as the first female Engineering Department Manager at Ad-Dhahiriya Municipality (2002–2021). Transitioned into private-sector consultancy and training (2021–present), specializing in FIDIC contract administration, dispute resolution, public procurement, and capacity building for engineers and municipal institutions. Proven track record in designing and delivering accredited Training of Trainers (TOT) programs, advising on tender processes, and supporting donor-funded local development initiatives. A recognized expert in translating technical engineering experience into institutional knowledge transfer.
