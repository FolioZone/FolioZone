About Abdallah

Abdullah Ibrahim Akkawy is a 27-year-old full-time Uber & delivery driver with over 8 years of professional driving experience. He is specialized in airport transfers, covering both arrivals and departures, and is actively involved in tourism-focused rides to Jordan's key tourist and historical sites.

Fluent in Arabic and conversational in English, Abdullah is known for his friendly attitude, clean car, punctuality, and dedication to providing a premium rider experience.
