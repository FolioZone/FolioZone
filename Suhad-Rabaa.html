Professional Summary

Civil Engineer and Municipal Development Leader with over 18 years of experience in urban planning, infrastructure development, and public projects management in complex and resource-constrained environments. I served as the Director of engineering and planning department at Al-Dhahiriya Municipality, and as the first female engineer in the history of both the municipality and the city of Al-Dhahiriya.

Throughout my career, I led and supervised the delivery of large-scale municipal infrastructure projects with direct socio-economic and developmental impact, including roads, utilities, public buildings, and community facilities. I also served as a member of operational planning committees, contributing to the preparation of execution and sustainability plans that ensured the effective translation of strategic visions into implementable projects.

I played a key leadership role in the successful implementation of the Historic Centers Revitalization Program, executed in partnership with the Belgian Government, Enabel, and the Palestinian Ministry of Local Government. Serving as the Project Manager for Al-Dhahiriya City, I managed a development project valued at USD 1.25 million, focused on urban heritage revitalization, infrastructure upgrading, and sustainable local development.

Currently, I am a Certified Engineering Contracts Arbitrator, an engineering and management consultant, and an entrepreneur in sustainable development and capacity building. I hold multiple professional certifications in arbitration, project management, and Training of Trainers (TOT), and I have actively contributed to institutional strengthening and workforce development programs.

My career is marked by pioneering leadership, women’s empowerment in engineering and public service, and the ability to transform municipal projects into practical tools for sustainable development.

I firmly believe that leadership is a responsibility before it is a position, and that true success is measured by long-term, people-centered impact. After an accomplished career in local government, I continue today to contribute through arbitration, consultancy, training, and entrepreneurship, at both national and international levels.
